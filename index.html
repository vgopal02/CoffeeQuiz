Instructions

1. Click on the "Let's Play" button

2. There are totally 5 questions to answer.

3. There is NO time limit to answer.

4. Your answer will highlight to either GREEN for correct or RED for incorrect.

5. You will get 10 points for each correct answer and will not lose points for incorrect answers.

6. Your current score will update as you play.

7. You may leave the game anytime by pressing the exit button.

8. Save your score and view it through the High Score section next time you visit.

Enjoy the quiz with a mug of your favourite brew!
